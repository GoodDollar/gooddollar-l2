## What We're Looking For

- **Broken flows** — transactions that fail, buttons that don't work
- **Confusing UX** — anything that made you pause or wonder what to do
- **Mobile issues** — layout problems on phones/tablets
- **Missing info** — unclear labels, missing confirmations, absent error messages
