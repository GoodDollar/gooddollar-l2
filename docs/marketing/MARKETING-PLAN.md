## 1. Executive Summary

GoodDollar L2 is positioned to disrupt traditional DeFi by embedding a verifiable, mandatory social impact mechanism (20% UBI contribution) into its core protocol fee structure. Our marketing strategy shifts the narrative from mere yield generation to **Impact Yield Generation**.

Over the next 90 days, we will execute a three-phased launch: **Awareness (Pre-Launch)** $\rightarrow$ **Validation (Testnet)** $\rightarrow$ **Adoption (Mainnet)**.

**Goal:** Achieve 10,000 unique active users on Testnet by Day 60, leading to a stable transaction volume of $500,000 USD equivalent on Mainnet within Q1 post-launch.

**Key Differentiator:** We are not just another L2; we are the *Socially Responsible L2*.

## 6. Launch Phases & Timeline

**Timeline:** 12 Weeks Total (Pre-Launch $\rightarrow$ Testnet $\rightarrow$ Mainnet)

### Phase 1: Awareness & Foundation (Weeks 1-4) - Pre-Launch
*   **Goal:** Build hype, educate on the UBI mandate, and onboard core technical users.
*   **Focus:** Content Pillars 1 & 3.
*   **Key Action:** Secure 3 major crypto podcasts for interviews.
*   **Metric Target:** 5,000 Discord members; 50+ GitHub contributors/watchers.

### Phase 2: Validation & Utility (Weeks 5-8) - Testnet Launch
*   **Goal:** Drive high transaction volume through controlled testing; stress-test protocols.
*   **Focus:** Content Pillars 2 & 4.
*   **Key Action:** Launch "Testnet Bounty Program" with rewards for high-volume, diverse protocol usage.
*   **Metric Target:** 10,000 unique Testnet wallets; $1M+ simulated transaction volume.

### Phase 3: Adoption & Scale (Weeks 9-12+) - Mainnet Launch
*   **Goal:** Achieve critical mass and establish sustained usage.
*   **Focus:** All Pillars.
*   **Key Action:** Major cross-promotion with established L2s/DeFi hubs. Launch the first official UBI impact report.
*   **Metric Target:** 100,000 Total Value Locked (TVL) across the 6 protocols; $500K+ USD transaction volume.
